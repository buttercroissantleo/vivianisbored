About Me

about-me.exc

hi stranger, i'm vivianisbored ♡ aka abi !

i'm a mexican-american, bay area native, la-based creative working across film, music, and visual media.

this site is my digital brain, part desktop, part archive, part moodboard, part visual diary.

i'm drawn to storytelling through experimental visuals, sound, and building worlds that feel playful, emotional, and a little chaotic.

i'm always making something, whether it's a song, a video, a zine, a collage, or just a mess of ideas in my notebook.

despite my personal aesthetic, horror is my favorite genre, and you can usually find me sipping oolong tea while writing or watching horror films.

click around the folders to see what i make! i hope you enjoy :D
